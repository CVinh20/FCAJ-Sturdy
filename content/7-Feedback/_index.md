> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at the company is highly professional and dynamic, yet very friendly and open. Everyone creates a comfortable atmosphere without putting negative pressure on interns, helping me integrate quickly. The well-equipped facilities and modern workspace are also a great motivation to improve productivity.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provided highly dedicated and thoughtful guidance, always ready to answer questions and encouraging me to take initiative in finding solutions. Additionally, the admin team was extremely supportive with administrative procedures, providing all necessary documentation and working tools, making the entire internship run smoothly.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Although most of the tasks required accessing new knowledge outside the theoretical curriculum at school, they were directly relevant to the career path of my academic major. Self-researching and applying these new technologies helped me expand my perspective and get hands-on experience with real industry trends.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship provided me with excellent opportunities to develop both technical and soft skills. I practiced self-learning new technologies, collaborating effectively in a team, and communicating or reporting professionally. The practical experiences and advice from my mentor have helped me shape a clearer career path for the future.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is highly positive, built on mutual respect. In work, everyone is serious, focused, and highly responsible. At the same time, the team spirit is clearly demonstrated as members are always ready to share knowledge and enthusiastically support each other to overcome challenges and achieve common goals.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides a clear internship allowance policy and offers flexible working hours, allowing me to balance university classes and work. Furthermore, being given the opportunity to participate in high-quality internal training sessions is a highly valuable benefit.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
  What satisfied me the most was the opportunity to experience working in a truly professional environment alongside highly talented seniors. Through this, I accumulated valuable practical lessons and skills that are not taught in university classrooms.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
  The company could provide more support and opportunities for interns to participate directly in specialized study sessions and technology networking events to enhance their practical experience.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
  Absolutely yes. This is an ideal environment for students who want to gain practical experience, learn new knowledge, and hone their skills in a professional, friendly, and highly supportive setting.
